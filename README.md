This project analyses HUL’s past 5 years (FY2021–FY2025) performance and uses it to forecast the next 5 years (FY2026–FY2030) by projecting the Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flows.

**Forecast Methodology** 

Revenue growth projected using historical 5-year CAGR of ~7.6% per annum
EBITDA margin ≈ 23–24%
PAT margin ≈ 16.5–17%
Operating Cash Flow projected at ~18–19% of revenue
Capex assumed low and stable, typical for an FMCG company.
Total assets projected to grow broadly in line with revenue.

**Key Forecast Results**

Revenue expected to reach ~₹90,000 crore by FY2030.
PAT expected to rise to ~₹14,500 crore by FY2030.
Operating Cash Flow expected to exceed ~₹16,000 crore by FY2030
Balance sheet continues to strengthen, with growing assets and low financial risk.

**Investment View**

Strengths: Strong brands, wide distribution, stable margins, high cash generation, consistent shareholder returns.
Risks: Slower demand growth, raw material price volatility, and regulatory or tax-related uncertainties.
**Overall**, HUL remains a financially stable, low-risk, long-term investment with predictable growth and strong fundamentals.

**Conclusion**

Based on historical data and conservative assumptions, HUL is expected to maintain steady growth, strong profitability, and healthy cash flows through 2030. The forecasts support a positive long-term outlook, making HUL suitable for defensive and long-term investors.
